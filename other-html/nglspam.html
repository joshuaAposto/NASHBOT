How to Use

1. Enter your username.

2. Choose the type of message you want to send from the dropdown menu.

3. Optionally, enter a custom message or leave it blank to use random messages.

4. Specify the number of messages you want to send (maximum 12).

5. Click the "Send" button to send your messages.
